Production Agentic AI Platform

LangGraph + MCP orchestration, deployed across 4 enterprise clients at Avaxia.

A multi-step agentic system that routes user goals across specialized tool-calling agents — connected to RAG retrieval, structured outputs, and external APIs. Every step is traced through Langfuse + LangSmith so retrieval failures, hallucinations, and tool errors surface in dashboards rather than user complaints.

EEG-Based ADHD Detection with Explainable AI

A clinician-validated pipeline built at RegimLab on EEG data from 180 subjects.

Raw 64-channel EEG was cleaned with Variational Mode Decomposition (VMD) and ICA, then projected onto multi-band spectrograms. An ensemble of CNN, LSTM, and EEGNet drives classification; per-prediction SHAP, LIME, and Integrated Gradients overlays give clinicians a biomarker-level explanation for every output.

Selected outcomes

  • 92% accuracy (F1: 0.91) — +6 pp over the prior lab baseline.
  • Feature space compressed from 320+ to 28 informative biomarkers.
  • Physician review time reduced by 22%; validated by 2 clinicians.

Stack

Python · PyTorch · EEGNet · CNN · LSTM · MNE · SHAP · LIME · Integrated Gradients · XGBoost · PCA · VMD · ICA
